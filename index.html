How to conquere burnout...

Assembly time: 1 hour
Relax rating: 100% relaxing

Feel like the deadlines are stressing you out and you can't seem to be able to write your articles? Don't worry, this has got you covered to make things as comfortable as they can be for you to get back to writing 5 star articles.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Your favorite mug
  • Lavender tea
  • A nice space other than your room
  • Calming lofi music
  • Most importantly - A change in perspective :)

  1. Grab your favorite mug.

    If the mug puts a smile on your face, then it'll help ease your mind from the troubles that comes with upcoming deadlines. Don't think about the time, you have time to grab your mug.

  2. Grab some lavender tea and start brewing water in a nearby kettle or pot.

    Lavender helps calms the nervous system, which promotes relaxation and helps with stresses and anxieties.

    A tea set with lavender tea with lavender flowers.

  3. Make a hole through the cork so that the bicycle pump can easily fit into it.

    Fix the pump tube to it with plasticine.

  4. Invert the bottle and cut three flippers of cardboard.
